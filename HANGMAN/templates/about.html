About Hangman Game

Welcome to the exciting world of Hangman! Hangman is a classic word-guessing game that has entertained people of all ages for generations. It's a game of wit, strategy, and a bit of luck.

Objective

The main objective of Hangman is to decipher a hidden word by suggesting letters. Players embark on a journey of deduction and word recognition to unveil the mystery word within a set number of attempts.

How to Play

Playing Hangman is a thrilling experience. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. A random word is carefully chosen, and its length is represented by blank spaces.
  2. Players take turns guessing letters by clicking on the provided letter options.
  3. If a guessed letter is part of the word, it is revealed in the corresponding blank space(s).
  4. Incorrect guesses contribute to the formation of a hangman figure, adding an element of suspense.
  5. The game continues until the entire word is uncovered, leading to victory, or the hangman figure is completed, resulting in a challenging defeat.

Scoring

Your prowess in Hangman is measured by your score, which is determined by the number of attempts it takes to unveil the word. Strive for accuracy and efficiency to achieve the highest score possible.
